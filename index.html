Our Mission

Community

To foster a vibrant community of aviation enthusiasts, promoting safety, knowledge sharing, and the joy of flight.

Skill-Development

To provide accessible and high-quality flight training, empowering members to reach their full potential as pilots.

Adventure-Driven

To ignite a passion for exploration and adventure through flight, offering opportunities to discover new horizons.

Inspire

To inspire, educate, and empower individuals to achieve their aviation dreams.

Our Services

We offer numerous program that cater to everyones needs.
If you dont see anything that suits your needs, don't hesitate to contact us

Flight Training

This program are for any aspiring individuals who are interested to become a Commercial Pilot. The program duration will last at least for 15 month.

License Conversion

If you currently posses foreign license and interested on converting into Indonesian's license, this program are the right one! This program consist of theory class and flying.

Joy Flight

There are no better feeling than flying, but the responsibility can be daunting. If you are interested to put a mark on you bucketlist, we offer a chance for anyone to fly around the country with our instructor.
