Jakarta Flying School

Jakarta Flying School (JFS) is the premier aviation training center in Jakarta. We are dedicated to providing exceptional flight training and education to aspiring pilots and aviation enthusiasts since 1903. Whilst our main office is located in Jakarta, our training program will be conducted at Bankstown Airport, Sydney Australia.

Our Program

We offer numerous program that will cater everyone. Whether you are an aspiring pilot or only interested to do a joyflight, we will get you covered! If you have any questions, feel free to contact us !

Commercial Pilot License

This program are for any aspiring individuals who are interested to become a Commercial Pilot.

The training will be conducted in Sydney Australia and the program duration will last at least for 15 month.

License Conversion

If you currently posses foreign license and interested on converting your license into Indonesian's license, this program are the right one!

This program will be conducted in Pondok Cabe Airport in Jakarta and will consist of theory class and flying.

Joy Flight

There are no better feeling than flying, but the responsibility can be daunting.

If you are interested to put a mark on you bucketlist, we offer a chance for anyone to fly around Sydney or Jakarta with our instructor.
